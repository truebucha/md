## Why delegate

- [ ] skill and career opportunities for team
- [ ] job satisfaction for team
- [ ] initiative and confidence for team
- [ ] commitment

### What not to delegate

- [ ] important ones
- [ ] tasks that you have no expertise in
- [ ] huge ones
- [ ] ones that was delegated to you recently
